President Trump announced on Monday evening at the White House Christmas party that his son Donald Jr. is engaged to be married to Palm Beach socialite, Bettina Anderson.

Bettina Anderson, 39, wore a red gown with a matching red sequin clutch.

“She said yes!” Don Jr. said after he proposed to Bettina Anderson.

“I just want to wish all of you guys an incredible holiday season and Merry Christmas and Happy New Year,” Don Jr. said. “And thank Bettina for that one word, ‘yes.'”

Bettina Anderson thanked President Trump and First Lady Melania for hosting a beautiful Christmas party.

“Wow! What a privilege it is to be here in the White House, Mr. President. Thank you so much for hosting this incredible party and to the First Lady…” she said.

She continued, “This has been the most unforgettable weekend of my life, and I get to marry the love of my life, and I feel like the luckiest girl in the world!”
